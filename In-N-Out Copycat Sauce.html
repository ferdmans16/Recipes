In-N-Out Copycat Sauce

Sauces

Prep Time: 2 min Total Time: 5 min Servings: 1 serving Source: tasty.co

Ingredients

½ cup mayonnaise

¼ cup ketchup

2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish

1 teaspoon sugar

1 teaspoon distilled white vinegar

Directions

In a medium bowl, combine the mayonnaise, ketchup, relish, sugar and vinegar and stir until well incorporated.

Serve on burgers or as a dip.

Enjoy!
